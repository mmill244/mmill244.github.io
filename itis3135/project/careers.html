STEM Careers

There are so many STEM careers to choose from and it can get overwhelming going through all the different possibilities. Some of the common careers include Engineering, Computer Science, Biology, and Mathematican.

Check out the list of careers below for more information!


Data Scientist

Data Scientist's analyzes and organizes large sets of data to find trends and manage data. Data Scientists are commonly hired to help businesses solve problems and run more efficiently.


Geoscientist

Geoscientist's work focuses on the earth's systems. They study the physical aspects of Earth including the composition, stucture, and processes.


Neuroscientist

Neuroscientists study the nervous system. They study and research behavorial effects of disorders, how the nervous system responds to traumas, and normal functions of the nervous system.


Oceanographer

Oceanographers study all aspect of the ocean. They study animals in that are in ocean, the chemisty of the water, and changes in differnt areas of the ocean.


Engineering

Engineers invent, design, analyze, build, and test machines. Different types of engineers include: mechanical, chemical, civil, electrical, management, and geotechnical.


Aeronautics

Aeronautic careers are based on the study of traveling through air. Careers in this field focus on the design and manufacture of flying machines.
